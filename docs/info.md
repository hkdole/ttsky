## How it works

ALU that supports the following operations:
* ADD
* SUB
* AND
* OR
* XOR
* NOT
* Shift left
* Shift right

Op codes are three bits and in order of presented (e.g. ADD is 000)
The system supports 5 bit numbers with overflow.
